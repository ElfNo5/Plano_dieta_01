THE SUNDAY BATCH

1

Cook the two big proteins

Pressure-cook the chicken and brown the beef. Shred / portion both while the oven runs.

2

Soak PTS, sear liver

Soy protein for chili & stroganoff soaks in stock; sear the liver hot and fast, then onions.

3

Oven runs the sweets

Egg muffins, quiche muffins and the week's pudins/bakes go in together — same heat, different trays.

4

Label by NIGHT, not just meal

Because dinners differ, write the weekday on each dinner pack so future-you grabs the right one.

FREEZE &amp; REHEAT

Cool first

Everything fully cool before freezing — a rotating week means more variety to keep straight, so label hard.

Flat packs

Single portions, flat. Group by weekday if you want to just grab-and-go.

Thaw ahead

Fridge overnight is best for the rotation — pull tomorrow's dinner + sweet the night before.

~1 month

Everything keeps ~a month. Cookies and bakes thaw fastest; pudins thaw sweetest overnight.
