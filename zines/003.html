THE 4 PLATES

Chicken breast, soy protein and eggs, almost no added fat, big piles of veg for volume. Cooked once Sunday, frozen, done. Nut-free top to bottom.

Meal 1 · Breakfast

EGG + SOY SCRAMBLE

ovos mexidos com PTS

  • 2 eggs (ovos)
  • 45 g dry PTS, rehydrated
  • onion + tomato, barely any oil
  • garlic · salt · pepper
≈270 kcal 35 g protein

↳ Soak PTS in hot stock 10 min, squeeze dry, scramble straight into the eggs. The stock makes it taste like more than it costs.

Meal 2 · Lunch

CHICKEN &amp; GREENS

peito de frango com verdura

  • 150 g cooked chicken breast
  • big pile of cabbage + greens
  • ½ tsp oil · paprika · garlic
≈290 kcal 46 g protein

↳ The leanest cheap protein there is — shred it into the greens so it stays juicy. Volume is your friend on a lean day.

Meal 3 · Dinner

SOY CHILI BOWL

chili de proteína de soja

  • 55 g dry PTS, rehydrated
  • tomato · onion · cabbage
  • cumin · paprika · ½ tsp oil
≈190 kcal 27 g protein

↳ Cook it like a chili minus the beans — keeps carbs down. Make it properly spicy so a lean week never gets boring.

THE SWEET ONE ✦

Meal 4 · The Sweet One

COCOA PUDIM

pudim proteico de cacau

  • 2 eggs + ½ banana
  • 40 g skim milk powder (desnatado)
  • cocoa (cacau) + sweetener
≈290 kcal 26 g protein

↳ Blend, pour into cups, water-bath bake 180 graus ~25 min. Chocolate that actually fits inside 1,300. No nuts, all protein.

THE SUNDAY BATCH

1

Soak the PTS

Cover the full week's soy protein in hot stock, 10 min, squeeze dry. First job, so it's ready when you need it.

2

Cook the chicken

Pressure-cook or poach the 1.3 kg of breast, shred it. Keep it moist — lean meat dries out fast.

3

Two soy pans

One scramble base, one spicy chili base. Season them apart so the week doesn't taste identical.

4

Bake the pudins + portion

Water-bath bake while everything cools. 28 servings, flat, labelled by day.

FREEZE &amp; REHEAT

Cool first

Fully cool before freezing so nothing turns to slush.

Flat packs

Single portions, flat. Faster thaw, easier stack.

Thaw ahead

Fridge overnight, or microwave from frozen ~3 min. Reheat lean chicken gently so it doesn't dry out.

~1 month

Eat within a month. Pudins thaw sweetest overnight in the fridge.
